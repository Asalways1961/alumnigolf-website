Getting Started

How to Enter

Four simple steps to get your team registered and ready to play.

1

Captain Registers

You'll enter your name, school, golf club, HNA handicap, and postal code. That's how we group you into a division with nearby teams for fair, competitive play.

📍 Accurate postal code = correct division placement
2

Invite Your Partner

Enter your partner's name, mobile, and email. They'll get an invite to complete their registration. Remember: both of you must be alumni of the same school.

🏫 Same school required — no exceptions
3

Confirm Eligibility & Pay

Confirm you're both eligible alumni with valid HNA handicaps and at least 18 years old. You'll agree to the Tournament Rules and Code of Conduct. Entry is R1,500 per player (R3,000 per team), or R2,800 if you pay for the team upfront—saving R200!

💰 Team payment saves you R200
4

You're In. Time to Play!

Once we verify your payment, you're officially in. You'll be placed in a division of 8 teams near you. Divisions are finalised after the registration period closes. Use the app or player portal to contact your opponents, arrange your match, and submit results.

Tournament Format

The Double-Elimination Bracket

Every division of 8 teams runs a double-elimination bracket. You get two chances before you're out.

R1

Round 1 — Winners & 2nd Chance

All 8 teams play. 4 winners go to the Winners Bracket. 4 losers go to the 2nd Chance Bracket.

14 Aug – 20 Sep 2026
R2

Round 2 — Two Brackets

Winners play Winners. 2nd Chance plays 2nd Chance. Losers in 2nd Chance are eliminated.

28 Sep – 18 Oct 2026
R3

Round 3 — Cross Bracket

2nd Chance Round 2 winners face Winners Bracket Round 2 losers. 2 undefeated teams get a bye.

26 Oct – 22 Nov 2026
R4

Semi-Finals

Round 3 winners face the two bye teams. Only 2 advance to the Division Final.

30 Nov 2026 – 15 Jan 2027
R5

Division Final

One match determines your Division Champion. The winner advances to the SA Final Qualifier.

1 – 28 Feb 2027
R6

SA Final Qualifier

• Divisional winners are paired geographically.
• Each match winner qualifies for the SA Final.

8 Mar – 11 Apr 2027

Eligibility

Who Can Play?

The tournament is open to all South African school alumni who meet the following requirements.

🏫

School Alumni

You must be a former pupil of a South African school — no longer enrolled as a learner in 2026 or later.

🎂

18 Years or Older

All participants must be at least 18 years of age at the time of registration.

⛳

Valid HNA Handicap

You must hold an active Golf RSA Handicapping Network Africa (HNA) handicap and be in good standing at your club.

🤝

Same School

Both players in a team must be alumni of the same school. Mixed, all-male and all-female teams all welcome.

📍

Any Location

Players from anywhere in South Africa can enter. Teams are grouped into local divisions by postal code.

♾️

Multiple Teams Per School

There is no limit on the number of teams from a single school. Get as many alumni involved as possible!

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What format is the golf played in? ▼
The tournament is played in Betterball Matchplay format throughout. Each team of two plays their better ball score on each hole against the other team's better ball. Handicaps are applied — 90% of Course Handicap, with the lowest handicap player set to scratch.
What if I can't find a partner from my school? ▼
You can join the Partner Pool! When registering you can select "I need help finding a partner". We will do our best to match you with another alumnus from the same school.
When and where do we play our matches? ▼
Teams contact each other via the app chat and agree on a date, time and venue within the round window. The home team books a GolfRSA-affiliated 18-hole or 9-hole course.
How are divisions assigned? ▼
Divisions are assigned by geo-plotting using your postal code — teams closest to each other are grouped together where possible. Each division has exactly 8 teams.
Are there refunds? ▼
Once a team is paid and included in the draw, no refunds are issued. Please ensure you are committed before completing payment.
