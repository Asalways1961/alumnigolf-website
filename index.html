About the Tournament

Where School Pride Meets the Fairway

The Alumni Schools Golf Challenge brings together former pupils of South African schools to celebrate shared traditions and friendly rivalry on the golf course.

🏌️

Betterball Matchplay Format

Two-player teams compete hole by hole in the classic betterball matchplay format, representing their school with pride.

📍

Geo-Plotted Divisions

Teams grouped into divisions of 8 by location — your postal code is of utmost importance when registering.

🏆

Path to the SA Final

Win the divisional play-offs and represent your school at a great event. Venue and final details to be announced soon.

📱

App & Web Managed

Manage everything through our dedicated app — launching soon.
