About the Tournament

Where School Pride Meets the Fairway

The Alumni Schools Golf Challenge is a fun, purely competitive golf tournament exclusively for former pupils of South African schools — bringing together alumni across the country to celebrate shared traditions and friendly rivalry.

🏌️

Betterball Matchplay Format

Two-player teams compete hole by hole in the classic betterball matchplay format — exciting, social and competitive.

📍

Geo-Plotted Divisions

Teams are grouped into divisions of 8 by location (postal code) — so you play against alumni near you before advancing nationally.

🏆

Path to the SA Final

Win your division, advance through the playoffs, and represent your school at the SA Final — a multi-day event in the Southern Cape.

📱

App & Web Managed

Manage scheduling, results and communication through our dedicated app launching 2 April 2026.
