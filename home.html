How It Works

Simple to join.
Exciting to play.

Four easy steps to take your place in the inaugural Alumni Schools Golf Challenge.

01
Register Online
Enter your team by entering your school and a few details.
02
Get Verified
Complete the verification for eligibility and you are registered.
03
Team Criteria
The 2 players in each team must be bona-fide Alumni of the same school.
04
Divisional Allocation
Division placement is determined by geo-plotting each team's postal code. Providing an accurate postal code ensures correct regional alignment, with 8 teams per division.

Eligibility

Who can play?

  • ✓
    Any former pupil of a South African high school
  • ✓

  • ✓

    Unlimited teams per school
